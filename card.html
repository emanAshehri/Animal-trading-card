LEOPARD

Most leopards are light coloured and have dark spots
on their fur. There are also black leopards, too,whose
spots are hard to see because their fur is so dark.

  • scientific name: Panthera pardus
  • avrage lifespan: 12-17 years
  • average speed: 58 km/h
  • habitats: forests, mountains

The leopard is the smallest of the four "big cats",the other three being the tiger, lion and jaguar. it is can be found in Africa, Central Asia, India and China...
